# Layout And Polish

Use this file when the structure feels weak even if the colors or components are acceptable.

## Layout Pass

Fix the page in this order:

1. Hero or opening frame
2. Information grouping
3. Section transitions
4. Repetition and rhythm
5. CTA placement

Ask these questions while editing:

- Is the most important action obvious in the first viewport?
- Does each section earn its height?
- Are adjacent sections visually distinct enough?
- Does the page alternate density, scale, or background treatment to avoid monotony?
- Do mobile stacks preserve hierarchy instead of becoming long undifferentiated lists?

## Component Polish

Tighten:

- heading wrap and line length
- padding consistency
- border and shadow logic
- hover, focus, pressed, and disabled states
- empty and loading states when relevant

## Motion Rules

Use animation to clarify, not decorate.

- Prefer fade, slide, scale, and reveal patterns with short durations.
- Stagger related content only when it supports reading order.
- Avoid perpetual motion on primary content.
- Keep page-load motion lighter on mobile.

## Responsive Rules

- Recompose layouts, do not only shrink them.
- Preserve a strong first screen on small devices.
- Reduce decorative layers when they crowd content.
- Keep tap targets and contrast accessible after styling changes.
